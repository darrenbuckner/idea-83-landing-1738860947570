Executive Overview

A conversational AI platform that integrates traditional financial management with personalized financial therapy, helping users align their emotions, goals, and financial strategies.

Problem Statement

Many individuals struggle with standard financial management tools that focus solely on numbers, ignoring the psychological aspects of spending, saving, and debt-management. This leaves a gap in services that address both financial best practices and the emotional nuances tied to financial decisions.

Solution Statement

By employing an AI-driven experience that offers ongoing personalized conversations, this solution bridges the gap between traditional financial management and financial therapy. It tailors advice based on users’ financial data as well as their emotional drivers, goals, and concerns, delivering a holistic strategy for better financial outcomes.
